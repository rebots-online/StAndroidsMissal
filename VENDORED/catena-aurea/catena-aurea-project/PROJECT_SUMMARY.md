### 🕸️ Sophisticated Knowledge Graph Structure

**Node Types:**
- **Gospel** (4): Matthew, Mark, Luke, John
- **Chapter** (89): One per Gospel chapter  
- **Gospel_Verse** (3,761): Individual verses with their text
- **Patristic_Source** (19): Church Fathers and authorities
- **Patristic_Narrative** (11,145): Commentary excerpts

**Relationship Types:**
- **Chapters**: Gospel → Chapter (which chapters belong to which Gospel)
- **Verses**: Chapter → Gospel_Verse (which verses are in each chapter)
- **Who_Wrote**: Patristic_Source → Patristic_Narrative (who wrote which commentary)
- **Commentary**: Gospel_Verse → Patristic_Narrative (which commentaries explain which verses)
