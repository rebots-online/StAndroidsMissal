## Google Play

### Short description (80 characters)

Traditional Latin Mass and Divine Office mapped for prayer, study, and discovery

### Full description

St. Android's Missal brings the 1962 Traditional Latin Mass and Divine Office into one
navigable, bilingual workspace.

Follow the Mass as a continuous route through its parts, move between Latin and English
texts, consult the perpetual liturgical calendar, and explore the Divine Office and
Sacred Scripture without losing your place. The Scripture Atlas surfaces canonical,
imagery, and parallel connections for study and free association. Journal and homily
tools keep personal notes and preparation alongside the liturgical day.

Features include:

- A subway-style map of the Mass and its liturgical sequence
- Bilingual Latin and English missal reading
- A perpetual calendar for the traditional liturgical year
- The Divine Office in the same navigable workspace
- Sacred Scripture browsing and thematic connections
- Journal, study-material, and homily-planning views
- Multiple visual themes for focused reading

St. Android's Missal is an independent educational and devotional reference. It is not
an official publication of the Holy See or of any diocese.
